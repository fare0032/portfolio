Jessica Fares Photography

Photography is the art and science of capturing images using light. It involves using a camera to take pictures of subjects, which can range from landscapes and nature to people, animals, and objects. Today, photography is a ubiquitous part of our lives, and digital cameras and smartphones have made it easier than ever for anyone to take high-quality photos.Photography is a powerful medium for storytelling, communication, and self-expression.

It allows us to capture memories, share experiences, and explore the world around us. Whether you're a professional photographer or just someone who enjoys taking photos for fun, learning the basics of photography can help you take better pictures and appreciate the art form more deeply.

The process of photography involves using a camera to capture an image, typically by focusing light onto a photosensitive surface or sensor. The resulting image can be processed and edited using various techniques to enhance its visual impact or artistic expression. Photography can be used for a variety of purposes, including journalism, advertising, fashion, art, and personal expression.
